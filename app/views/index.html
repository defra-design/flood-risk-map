How to draw an area

Using a mobile phone or tablet

  1. Press 'Start drawing'
  2. Choose a starting point for your area and adjust the map to move this into the circle
  3. Press 'Add point'
  4. Repeat step 2 and 3 until you have marked all the edges of your area
  5. Press 'Finish area'

Using a mouse

  1. Click 'Start drawing'
  2. Choose a starting point for your area and click to add a point
  3. Repeat step 2 until you have marked all the edges of your area
  4. Click on the first point again to finish

Using a keyboard

  1. Select 'Start drawing'
  2. Decide on a starting point for your area
  3. Use Shift + ⇧ ⇦ ⇩ ⇨ or + − to adjust the map, moving the point into the circle
  4. Select 'Add point'
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have marked all the edges of your area
  6. Select 'Finish area'
